Bike Related Dashboard MVP

Welcome to the MVP of our Group Project website!

This website is designed to be a dashboard to present relevant information at a glance for different types of users who love biking. You can navigate the website via the bar above ^.

On our MVP website, we have information relevant for:

  • bikers
  • bike store owners
  • citizens concerned about safety

We also have a link to more information about our team. We are coding novices so we are focused on functionality over form!

We made this website because we think biking is important. It is a way to stay healthy and protect the environment.

There are a lot of great resources on the Internet to learn even more about biking, such as The League of American Bicyclists
